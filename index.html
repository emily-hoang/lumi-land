Sparkle And The Rainbow Promise

Fantasy & Magic Picture Book

Sparkle the unicorn loves chasing butterflies in the sunny meadows, but one day her adventure leads her deep into the forest when a lonely, mischievous unicorn named Shadow lures her with promises of lollies and ice cream. Lost and frightened, Sparkle is soon found by her loving parents, who search tirelessly through the woods. In the forest under a glowing rainbow, Sparkle is reunited with her family, and Shadow reveals his true intention—he only wanted a friend.

With kindness and forgiveness, Sparkle invites Shadow to join her family, and as he regains his rainbow colors, he discovers he is never alone again. The story ends with the three unicorns walking home together, hearts full of love, friendship, and the magic of a safe and happy home.

What You'll Discover:

  • The importance of family and staying safe: Sparkle learns not to wander too far from her parents.
  • Friendship and empathy: Shadow only wanted a friend, showing children the value of understanding others' feelings.
  • Forgiveness and kindness: Sparkle and her parents forgive Shadow and welcome him into their family.
  • Making wise choices: The story teaches children to think carefully before following promises that seem too good to be true.

About the Author

Emily Hoang

Magical bedtime stories with gentle lessons for growing hearts

Emily is a software engineer and proud mother of two wonderful children. She deeply understands the importance of nurturing young minds with stories that are both magical and meaningful, teaching simple yet valuable life lessons from early childhood.

Inspired by her own experiences as a parent, she created this website to share the children's books she has crafted using her imagination, along with the help of innovative AI tools.

Each book offers a gentle, enchanting story designed to captivate little ones at bedtime while planting the seeds for a joyful and thoughtful life.
